General Tax Information

Income Tax

Income tax is a direct tax imposed on individuals and businesses based on their earnings. It's a progressive tax system, meaning higher earners pay a higher percentage of their income in taxes.

Property Tax

Property tax is a tax assessed on real estate holdings. It's typically based on the value of the property and is used to fund local services such as schools, roads, and public safety.

Sales Tax

Sales tax is a consumption tax imposed by the government on the sale of goods and services. The tax is typically calculated as a percentage of the purchase price and is collected by the seller.

Other Taxes

Other taxes may include various levies such as capital gains tax, inheritance tax, or specific local taxes. These can vary significantly depending on the jurisdiction and individual circumstances.
